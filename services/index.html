Core Services

Medical waste programs built around real facility workflows

United Medical Waste supports healthcare facilities with regulated medical waste collection, sharps management, pharmaceutical waste, compliance training, and waste stream audits.

Programs can include container planning, pickup scheduling, transport documentation, staff training support, and records that help administrators keep waste files organized.

Some facilities also need additional support such as document destruction, emergency cleanup questions, or a waste audit before they change service. The team can review those needs during the quote process.

Common service needs

  • Regulated medical waste and red bag pickup
  • Sharps container delivery, exchange, and disposal
  • Pharmaceutical waste stream review
  • OSHA, HIPAA, DOT, RCRA, and Bloodborne Pathogens training topics
  • Waste stream audits and documentation review
